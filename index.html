All about the Mets!

Basic info

The New York Mets are a baseball team in the National League East. They play at Citi Field in Flushing Queens. They have been a team since 1962. They have won the world series twice, in 1969 versus the Baltimore Orioles, and in 1986 versus the Boston Red Sox. The Mets have many players incducted into the hall of fame such as Mike Piazza and Tom Seaver
